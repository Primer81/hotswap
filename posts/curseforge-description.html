HotSwap

HotSwap is a small, simple mod for Minecraft Forge which adds hotkeys and mouse controls to quickly switch hotbars from your inventory. You can switch out either the selected slot or the entire hotbar.

HotSwap can be a lighter, simpler substitute for other inventory management mods,with minimal impact on the rest of the game.

Controls

  • J and K (default): cycle the current slot down or up
  • H and L (default): cycle the hotbar down or up
  • Ctrl + MWheel: cycle the current slot down or up
  • Ctrl + Alt + MWheel: cycle the hotbar down or up
